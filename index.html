Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most common questions about investing in LuxuryHomes. If you need more information, feel free to reach out.

1. How much is a share?

Each share costs $26. You can purchase as many shares as you like to increase your profit potential.

2. How do I buy shares?

Buying shares is simple! Follow these steps:

  • Visit our official website: LuxuryHomes
  • Click on the "Buy Shares" button.
  • You will be redirected to gmail, express interst in buying shares.
  • One of our staff will reach out and guide you in secure payment processing via Bank or PayPal.
  • Once payment is complete, your account will be created and logins sent via email, your shares and projected profits will be displayed in your dashboard.

3. How can I opt out?

You are free to opt out at any time. Simply withdraw your profits, and we will process your payout based on the following:

  • If your balance is less than $10,000, payments will be sent via PayPal.
  • If your balance is $10,000 or more, payments will be processed via bank transfer.

No penalties or hidden charges—your investment is always in your control.

4. What if the company makes a loss?

While our real estate investments are highly profitable, we understand the importance of security. In the unlikely event of a loss, rest assured that we are fully insured, and all shareholders will have their investment refunded in full.

5. How can I raise a complaint or contact you?

The best way to reach us is via email. We ensure timely responses to all shareholder inquiries. For any questions or support, please contact us at:
